Dave Lauby

Dave Lauby is a playwright, actor, teacher and director with nearly 40 years of experience in the professional theatre. The summer of 2013 saw the world premier of his comedy "The Great McAnigan", produced by Atlanta's The Collective Project (in association with ByrdBrane Productions), a production which garnered rave reviews and two Suzi Award nominations. In June of 2014 his comedy "Trash Day" received its world premier at Atlanta's Out Of Box Theatre, a production which earned the 2014 Metro Atlanta Theatre (MAT) Award for Best Original Work. In the autumn of 2015 Dave's newest play, "The Legend Of Big Mama And The Titless Wonder", will be staged in a metro Atlanta theatre space TBD. Four more Dave Lauby plays- "The Scientific Method", "Lewis & Clark and the Death Metal Motorcycle Tribe", "Softies", and "SingleWide"- await future production, and a fifth, "How To Lose Everything", is the current work-in-progress.
