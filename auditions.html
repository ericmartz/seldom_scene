Now Hiring: Technical and Artistic staff for "Lewis & Clark and the Death Metal Motorcycle Tribe"

The process is now underway to staff a variety of technical and artistic positions for The Seldom Scene Theatre's inaugural production, "Lewis & Clark and the Death Metal Motorcycle Tribe," tentatively slated for February of 2016. Each position will receive competitive pay, negotiable per position (all non-union). At this point we're most interested in filling the following roles:

  • Technical Director
  • Production Stage Manager
  • Sound Design
  • Costume Design
  • Lighting Design

Please click the "Coming Next" and "Mission" tabs on this website to learn more about "Lewis & Clark and the Death Metal Motorcycle Tribe" and Seldom Scene Theatre in general. If you're interested in any position and would like an interview or to learn more, please either email Dave Lauby at byrdbrane2@yahoo.com or click the "Contact" tab and send a contact request from this website. Here's to working together!
